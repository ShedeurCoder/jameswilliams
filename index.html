About James Williams

James Williams is the mayor of Legoland City who is currently running for vice president of Toyland. As a part of the Progress Party, he has brought many reforms as mayor and will continue until he retires. Born in Legoland City, Legoland in a lower-middle class family, he was raised to be humble and to never take things for granted.

His Master's in political science allows him to understand politics and how to please everyone whilst still making progress. He has made many compromises with other parties to ensure the people get served, for he believes those who govern are governed by the people, and no person shall be left unsatisfied. He has always wanted to serve his country, helping his great nation progress as one. Now at his position, he can achieve his dreams.

James Williams for VP!

James Williams is running as vice president of Toyland with his friend and former governor of Legoland, Jorgen Wurtz. We plan to bring progress to not just Legoland, but to the whole nation of Toyland. We promise we will work our hardest to deliver you your needs and to deliver the country greatness. Don't hesitate, vote Wurtz - Williams 2022!
